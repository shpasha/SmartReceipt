# SmartReceipt

> 100% vibe-coded product

An easy way to split a restaurant bill with friends — no calculators, no notes-app spreadsheets, no awkward "wait, what did you order again?"

🔗 **Try it:** [shnyakin.com/receipt](https://shnyakin.com/receipt)

## How it works

1. **Snap the receipt.** Upload a photo of the bill — the app reads the items, prices, tax, and service charge for you.
2. **Create a room.** You get a short 5-character code and a link — share it with your group.
3. **Everyone picks their own.** Friends join with the code and tap what they had. Shared a dish? Just split it.
4. **See the totals live.** Each person's total updates in real time. Tax is split fairly by what you ordered, service charge is shared equally among everyone who ate.

## Why

- Nobody overpays for someone else's steak.
- No retyping items from the receipt.
- Everyone sees the same thing — no arguments, no recounting.
- Works right from your phone, nothing to install.
